### What is Data Analytics?

Data analytics is the process of extracting meaningful information
from data to support decision making.

### Why businesses need data?

Businesses need data to:

- understand their performance
- identify problems and opportunities
- make informed decisions

### Key Terms

- **Data**: raw facts and numbers without interpretation
- **Information**: processed data that has meaning
- **Insight**: a meaningful conclusion derived from information
- **KPI**: a metric used to measure business performance
